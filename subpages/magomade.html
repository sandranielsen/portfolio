Concept

MagoMade is a creative universe, that is aimed at making crocheting and knitting easy and more accessible for everyone - from beginners to the the more skilled - through detailed guides and simple patterns.

Project Description

During the 3rd semester of my web development top-up I interned in my own business, MagoMade. The idea and concept behind MagoMade arose a year prior but it was during my internship MagoMade was truly brought to life. The goal of the project was to design and develop a sales platform in Shopify and create a SoMe marketing strategy to bring traffic to the webshop.

The concept was additionally refined during my bachelor thesis which also revolved around MagoMade. Here I migrated the sales platform from Shopify to Headless Shopify using the React-based framework Gatsby to code the frontend while Shopify still facilitated the backend and product databases. Furthermore I converted the webshop to a progressive web app.
